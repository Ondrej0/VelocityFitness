TRAIN THE
FIGHTER IN YOU

Velocity Fitness is a leading martial arts and fitness club in Bristol and the surrounding areas. With three locations, we are known for our positive energy, rain or shine. Our foundation is built on experienced and inspirational coaches. We offer martial arts, fitness, boxing, personal training, and open gym sessions. We pride ourselves on fostering an inclusive community. Our dedicated staff is committed to helping you become the best version of yourself, improving your fitness, lifestyle, and confidence.

MARTIAL ARTS

Unlock your potential with our martial arts classes, where discipline meets empowerment. Learn self-defense techniques, improve your focus, and build confidence under the guidance of experienced instructors. FITNESS

Boost your energy and strength with our comprehensive fitness classes, designed to challenge and inspire all levels. Our expert trainers guide you through high-intensity workouts and targeted exercises that enhance endurance, build muscle, and promote overall wellness. FLEXIBILTY

Enhance your range of motion and prevent injury with our flexibility classes. These amazing sessions focus on stretching and mobility exercises that improve posture, reduce tension, and increase your overall flexibility. Perfect for all fitness levels, our classes help you move more freely and feel revitalized in your daily activities.
